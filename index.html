About Us

TEMAmost Nig Ltd was incorporated with the Cooperate Affairs Commission (CAC) of Nigeria, in September 2017(check RC number on our logo above) with a diversified structure, which includes rendering Professional services in Civil infrastructure and property development engineering, Architectural Designs, Building Drawings, Real Estate Agents, Real Estate development and management, as well as Interior Designs, Property Damage Remediation and Reconstruction, and Consultative Services, Supply of General Construction materials
